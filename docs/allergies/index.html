Allergies

Here's a list of things I'm allergic to:

  • Peanuts
  • Chickpeas
  • Lentils
  • Edamame
  • Sesame oil
  • Tahini
  • Sesame seeds
  • Poppy seeds
  • Green peas
  • Pea protein, soy protein
  • Mung beans
  • Hazelnuts
  • Cashews
  • Eggs (scramble, sunny side up, etc.)

Here's a list of things I'm not allergic to:

  • Tofu
  • Tempeh
  • Almonds
  • Pecans
  • Pistachios
  • Black beans
  • Pinto beans
  • Kidney beans
  • Eggs in baked goods
