About me

I'm a software developer with a Bachelor of Computer Science from the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Canada.

I'm currently working as a senior developer at Faire.

In the past, I've worked at Datadog, Zeitspace, Shopify Plus, and Boltmade (since acquired by Shopify).

I'm a Recurse Center alum, having attended a one-week mini-batch in September 2020.

In my spare time, I like to read. My favourite author is Ursula K. Le Guin. I sing, play the keyboard and the electric bass, and produce electronic music. I also write for and act in amateur theatre productions.
